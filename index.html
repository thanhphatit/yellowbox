About This Repository

This repository serves as a single home for every project, tool, or automation script I’ve built. Instead of managing dozens of small repos, everything is gathered here with a clean, categorized folder structure. Each directory represents a standalone module — whether it’s a Kubernetes chart, a Bash/Helm utility, or an experimental app.

The goal: clarity, reusability, and full ownership.

Structure Overview

  • /apps/ – Independent or experimental applications.
  • /tools/ – CLI utilities, DevOps scripts, and helper functions.
  • /charts/ – Helm charts, operators, and cluster deployment assets.
  • /scripts/ – Standalone shell or Python automation tools.
  • /docs/ – Internal notes, design decisions, or technical overviews.

Philosophy

Everything here is crafted with a focus on automation, observability, and resilience. It’s where experiments evolve into stable reusable assets. I build once, reuse everywhere.

Notes

The domain itblognote is just a namespace – it’s not tied to any specific service or system. Think of it as a “marker” for this unified workspace, not a brand or product.
